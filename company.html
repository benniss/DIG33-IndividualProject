The Company

Brand Info

Scavi & Ray Prosecco is Italy’s premium Prosecco, typified by its elegant design and outstanding grape quality; cultivated in a breath-taking sun-drenched landscape with perfect soil conditions. The grapes are harvested by hand with tradition, passion and diligence. A premium, international brand that supports London Fashion Week, Amsterdam Fashion Week and major lifestyle brands across the globe – chosen by innovators and professionals and the perfect addition to any special occasion.

Product Range

The Scavi & Ray range includes the classic Scavi & Ray Spumante; the figurehead of the brand, a wonderfully sweet Rosato and the elderflower infused Hugo as well as a unique non-alcoholic Prosecco.
